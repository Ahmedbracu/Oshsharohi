About OSHSHAROHI

To become a global leader in student-led automotive innovation, fostering a culture of excellence where engineering meets sustainability. We design, build, and race high-performance vehicles while empowering students with hands-on technical skills.

OSHSHAROHI represents more than just a racing team, it's a movement to push the boundaries of what students can achieve when given the right tools, mentorship, and opportunity to excel.

Our Mission

We are committed to nurturing the next generation of automotive engineers by providing hands-on experience in designing, building, and racing high-performance vehicles. Our mission extends beyond the racetrack, we aim to inspire innovation that will shape the future of sustainable mobility.

  • Develop cutting-edge electric and hybrid racing technology
  • Foster interdisciplinary collaboration among students
  • Compete at the highest levels of Formula Student globally
  • Build industry partnerships for real-world experience
  • Contribute to sustainable mobility research
