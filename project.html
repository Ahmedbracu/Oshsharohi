OS-26 (OSH-V1)

Our flagship formula car designed for Formula Student Germany. Featuring advanced aerodynamics, lightweight carbon fiber composite construction, and a high-efficiency powertrain with proprietary battery management system.

  • Carbon Fiber Monocoque Chassis
  • Active Aerodynamics Package
  • Adjustable Damping Suspension
  • High-Efficiency Powertrain
  • Optimized Cooling System
  • Custom Battery Management System
  • Regenerative Braking Technology
  • Data Acquisition & Telemetry

Engineering Excellence

Chassis & Aerodynamics

The OS-26 features a custom-designed carbon fiber monocoque chassis that provides exceptional rigidity while minimizing weight. Our aerodynamics package has been developed through extensive CFD simulations and wind tunnel testing.

The active aerodynamics system adjusts in real-time based on speed and cornering forces, maximizing downforce when needed while reducing drag on straights.

Powertrain & Electronics

Our high-efficiency electric powertrain delivers over 80 HP with instant torque delivery. The proprietary battery management system ensures optimal cell balancing and thermal regulation for consistent performance.

Advanced telemetry systems provide real-time data for driver feedback and post-race analysis, enabling continuous performance optimization.
